The Museum of Romance

Concept Video
2019

Role: Production Designer, Set Designer

Directed by Matthew Purpura
Produced by Xinwen Chen

Description: A virtual reality concept in which participants have the ability to interact in various romantic story lines originating from a multitude of cultural and societal backgrounds via VR headsets and controllers. Made for the Immersive Media Challenge grant from the Entertainment Technology Center at the University of Southern California.
