About Me


Jay Goldstuck is an almost-adult from Johannesburg, South Africa. Passionate about film, Jay moved to Los Angeles, California to study Film and TV Production at the University of Southern California and to pursue a career in the industry. Jay is committed to social justice and aims to bring about institutional change with the help of art and filmmaking. As well as being a student, Jay is an experienced editor and prop maker, and is currently branching into the fields of world building and design. After graduating, Jay intends to pursue a career in both production design and documentary filmmaking.
